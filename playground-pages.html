分块
The researchers
who had been studying the long-term effects
of microplastics on marine ecosystems
published a paper
that contradicts the widely held assumption

为什么难读
主语和动词之间塞了一个很长的定语从句（who...ecosystems），读到"published"的时候已经忘了前面说的是谁。后面又用"that"连续套了两层：先说论文否定了什么，再说那个假设的具体内容。
学会表达
contradict the widely held assumption that...
否定一个普遍认知："This contradicts the assumption that AI will replace all jobs."
